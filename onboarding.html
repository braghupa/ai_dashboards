Synthetic data notice: All figures shown in this platform are computer-generated for demonstration purposes only. No real patient, staffing, equipment, or operational data from VCH or PHC systems is used. Do not use for clinical or operational decisions.

Using the filters
Health authority
VCH (8 sites) or PHC (SPH, MSJ), or view all 10 together
Site
Drill into VGH, UBC, RHS, LGH, SGH, SSH, QGH, WHC, SPH, or MSJ
Modality
Isolate X-Ray, MRI, CT, US, Echo, Nuc Med, or IR
Period
2023, 2024, or YTD 2025. YoY delta updates automatically
Patients / Exams
Patients = unique individuals. Exams = total procedures performed
Reading the colour indicators
Green — on target
Utilisation <80% · Waitlist <30 days · Equipment in Good or Fair condition
Amber — watch
Utilisation 80–90% · Waitlist 30–60 days · Equipment Aging
Red — action needed
Utilisation >90% · Waitlist >60 days · Equipment Critical
What each tab shows
Overview
Volume by site, population vs visitor demand, waitlist by modality — your starting point
Demand vs capacity
Modality utilisation RAG bars and a 12-month seasonal trend line
Alerts
Auto-generated flags for critical equipment, aging units under load, and waitlist breaches
Site detail
Cross-site comparison — volume, population, FTEs, waitlist, and critical equipment
Equipment
Unit count, age, utilisation, and status per site and modality
Labour FTEs
Radiologists, technologists, and admin per site with 2025 gap projections
Waitlist
Site × modality matrix of wait times in days against benchmarks
Forecast
2025 demand forecast with confidence interval and FTE gap table
Modality view
Utilisation heatmap and dual-axis waitlist vs capacity chart
AI insights assistant
Ask questions in plain language
The AI assistant reads your active filters and all visible data to answer questions about capacity pressure, waitlist risk, equipment priorities, and staffing gaps. It tailors answers to your selected role. Try asking:
Which sites have the highest MRI pressure? Does Richmond justify a new CT unit? Where are my FTE gaps for 2025? Which equipment needs urgent replacement? What's driving the August volume spike?
